What is Cube Root?

The cube root of a number is a value that, when multiplied by itself three times, gives the original number.

∛64 = 4

How It Works

  • Uses inverse operation of cubing (x³)
  • Works with positive and negative numbers
  • Calculated using: ∛n = n^(1/3)
