FD Formula (Simple Interest)

M = P + (P √ó r √ó t)

Where:

M = Maturity Value

P = Principal Amount

r = Annual Interest Rate (decimal)

t = Time in years

RD Formula (Compound Interest)

M = P √ó [( (1 + i)^n - 1 ) / i ] √ó (1 + i)

Where:

M = Maturity Value

P = Monthly Deposit

i = Monthly Interest Rate (annual rate / 12)

n = Number of Months
